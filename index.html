electerm is a terminal/ssh/sftp client(mac, win, linux) based on electron/node-pty/xterm and many other modules, I hope it works as a combination of guake and xshell.

features

  • work as a terminal or ssh/sftp client(similar to xshell)
  • hotkey to bring window back to front(simliar to guake, default is ctrl + 2)
  • support multi platform(mac, win, linux)
